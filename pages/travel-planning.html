Common Questions

We charge a small initial deposit to start the design process, which is fully credited towards the final cost of your booking.

How much input do I have?

As much as you want! Some clients give us a blank slate, others have specific lists. We work collaboratively with you.

Can you plan for large groups or families?

Yes, we specialize in multi-generational family trips and large group logistics.
